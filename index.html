Compute Instances (AWS EC2)

Virtual servers (instances) are the workhorses of your application. Costs vary based on performance requirements (CPU, RAM) and commitment.

  • On-Demand: Pay-per-hour for flexibility. (e.g., a basic t2.micro instance is ~‚Çπ0.97/hour).
  • Reserved Instances: Offer significant discounts (up to 72%) for committing to 1 or 3 years.
  • Spot Instances: Up to 90% off On-Demand, suitable for fault-tolerant workloads.
